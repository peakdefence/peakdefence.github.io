Client Success Security Consultant

February 15, 2025

Mission Connection

As a Client Success Security Consultant, you’ll be at the forefront of Peak Defence’s mission to transform security management through the perfect integration of People, Process, and Technology. You’ll work directly with clients to implement our methodology, ensuring they achieve meaningful security outcomes rather than just checking compliance boxes.

Role Overview

Unlike traditional consultants who primarily give advice, you’ll be hands-on in implementing solutions, driving adoption of our methodology, and ensuring clients achieve measurable security improvements. You’ll work across the entire security lifecycle: from initial assessment through implementation, optimization, and ongoing management.

What You’ll Do

Strategic Security Implementation

  • Embed with clients to understand their security challenges in depth and become a trusted partner in their security journey
  • Translate security frameworks (ISO 27001, NIS2, SOC 2, DORA) into practical implementation plans aligned with the Peak Defence methodology
  • Guide clients through security program development using our structured approach of strategic, tactical, and operational planning

Technical Security Enablement

  • Configure and optimize the Wingman platform to address each client’s unique security needs
  • Help clients leverage the platform’s AI and graph database capabilities to gain unprecedented visibility into their security posture
  • Implement security controls and processes that demonstrate real protection rather than just documentation

Client Success Leadership

  • Own client relationships and serve as their primary advocate within Peak Defence
  • Identify opportunities to expand platform adoption and service engagement based on evolving client needs
  • Measure and communicate the concrete security improvements achieved through our partnership

Continuous Improvement

  • Gather client feedback to inform product development and methodology refinement
  • Develop reusable implementation patterns that can benefit all clients
  • Contribute to our knowledge base of security best practices and implementation techniques

What You’ll Bring

  • 5+ years of hands-on information security experience, particularly in governance, risk, and compliance
  • Experience implementing security frameworks such as ISO 27001, SOC 2, NIST, or similar
  • Strong technical foundation with the ability to understand security controls across various technology domains
  • Excellent communication skills with the ability to translate complex security concepts for diverse audiences
  • Problem-solving mentality with a focus on implementation rather than just assessment
  • Experience with security tools and platforms (GRC tools, SIEM, vulnerability management, etc.)
  • Security certifications such as CISSP, CISM, or framework-specific certifications (preferred)

Why Peak Defence

  • Opportunity to transform how organizations approach security management
  • Work with cutting-edge AI and graph database technology applied to security challenges
  • Collaborative team environment focused on meaningful security outcomes
  • Competitive compensation with rewards tied to client success
  • Clear career progression path with opportunities to grow technical and leadership skills
  • Remote-friendly work environment with flexible scheduling

How We Work

At Peak Defence, we follow a structured yet flexible methodology that combines strategic quarterly planning with agile two-week cycles. We believe in team autonomy, end-to-end ownership, and building for real-world impact. Our work manifesto emphasizes sustainable pace, continuous learning, and focusing on what truly matters for security effectiveness.
