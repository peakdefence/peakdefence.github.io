Join Our Mission to Transform Security

Peak Defence is changing how organizations manage security through professional services and our AI-powered platform, Wingman. We combine cutting-edge artificial intelligence, advanced graph database technology, and human expertise to deliver solutions that are smarter, more efficient, and more effective than traditional approaches.

Why Join Us

  • Innovation at the Core: Work with cutting-edge technologies including AI, machine learning, and graph databases
  • Meaningful Impact: Help organizations protect their most valuable assets and build more resilient security programs
  • Growth-Focused Culture: Develop your skills in a fast-moving environment that values continuous learning
  • Product-Led Approach: Contribute to a platform that’s changing how the industry thinks about security
  • Collaborative Team: Work alongside passionate professionals who value teamwork and knowledge sharing

What We’re Solving

For Our Customers

We’re addressing the most pressing challenges in security management:

  • Overwhelming Complexity: We simplify security by using AI to reveal hidden patterns and relationships that traditional platforms miss
  • Resource Limitations: Our virtual CISO services and automation tools help teams achieve more with less
  • Cognitive Overload: Our machine learning algorithms reduce alert fatigue by filtering out noise and highlighting what truly matters
  • Compliance Burdens: We automate evidence collection and framework mapping to make compliance manageable
  • Disconnected Systems: Our platform integrates security elements into a unified system for comprehensive visibility

For Society

In the bigger picture, we’re working to:

  • Make advanced security accessible to more organizations
  • Reduce the global impact of cyber threats through proactive risk management
  • Close the cybersecurity talent gap through AI-augmented expertise
  • Promote a more secure digital ecosystem for everyone

How We Work

At Peak Defence, we follow a structured yet flexible methodology that combines strategic quarterly planning with agile two-week cycles. We believe in team autonomy, end-to-end ownership, and building for real-world impact. Our work manifesto emphasizes sustainable pace, continuous learning, and focusing on what truly matters for security effectiveness.
