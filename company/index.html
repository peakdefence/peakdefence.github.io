About Peak Defence

Our mission, vision, and the team behind our innovative security platform.

Our Mission

Peak Defence exists to transform how organizations manage security through the perfect integration of People, Process, and Technology. We believe security should be intelligent, accessible, and effective for every organization.

Our Story

Founded by a team of security leaders willing to challenge the status quo and outdated approaches, Peak Defence was born from a simple observation: security management was broken. Organizations were drowning in manual processes, struggling with disconnected tools, and failing to achieve meaningful security outcomes.

Our founders set out to build something fundamentally different - a platform that combines artificial intelligence, advanced graph database technology, and human expertise to deliver security solutions that are smarter, more efficient, and more effective than traditional approaches.

Our Approach

Peak Defence takes a balanced, three-pillar approach to security management:

1. People-Centric

We believe that technology alone can’t solve security challenges. Our platform and methodology are designed to empower security teams, not replace them. We focus on:

  • Augmenting human expertise with AI assistance
  • Reducing cognitive overload through intelligent automation
  • Creating intuitive interfaces that security professionals actually want to use
  • Providing expert guidance alongside our technology

2. Process-Driven

Strong security requires structured, consistent processes. Our approach includes:

  • Methodology based on proven security frameworks
  • Automated workflows that ensure nothing falls through the cracks
  • Continuous improvement through data-driven insights
  • Simplified compliance through built-in best practices

3. Technology-Enabled

Our cutting-edge technology platform brings it all together:

  • AI-powered analysis and decision support
  • Graph database architecture for comprehensive relationship mapping
  • Seamless integration with existing security tools
  • Real-time visibility across your entire security landscape

Our Values

Excellence Without Compromise

We believe in doing things right, not just doing them. This means taking the time to build robust, thoughtful solutions rather than rushing to market with incomplete products.

Radical Transparency

We are honest about what we (and our technology) can and cannot do. We build trust through clear communication, open sharing of information, and admitting when we don’t have all the answers.

Customer Obsession

We measure our success by our customers’ success. Every decision we make starts with the question: “How does this help our customers achieve better security outcomes?”

Continuous Innovation

The security landscape never stops evolving, and neither do we. We’re committed to pushing boundaries, challenging assumptions, and constantly improving our platform.

Meaningful Security

We focus on real security improvements, not just checking boxes. Our goal is to help organizations build genuinely resilient security programs that stand up to real-world threats.
